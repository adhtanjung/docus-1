# Workforce Operations

Drive organizational efficiency with automated day-to-day workforce management.

## Attendance Tracking

HAER provides real-time visibility into workforce presence:

- **Clock-In/Out**: Flexible methods (Web, Mobile, Integration).
- **Location Awareness**: Optional geo-fencing for remote or site-based teams.
- **Overtime Calculation**: Automatic calculation based on shift rules and actual hours.

## Work Schedules & Shifts

Manage complex working patterns:

- **Shift Definitions**: Create reusable shift patterns (e.g., Night Shift, 9-to-5, Rotational).
- **Roster Planning**: Assign schedules to individual employees or entire groups.
- **Flexibility**: Support for break times, grace periods, and schedule changes.

## Leave Management

Automated leave workflows ensure coverage and fairness:

- **Policy Engine**: Define entitlement rules based on seniority, role, or location.
- **Approval Workflow**: Multi-level approval chains with automatic notifications.
- **Balance Tracking**: Real-time accrual and deduction tracking.
- **Calendars**: Integrated view of team availability.

## Payroll & Salary

(Briefly mentioned as it's part of the core tags):

- **Compensation History**: Track salary changes over time with historic accuracy.
- **Components**: Manage base pay, allowances, and deductions.
- **Integration Ready**: Exportable data for common payroll engines.
