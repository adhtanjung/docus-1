# Product Overview

HAER is a next-generation **Human Capital Management (HCM) System** built to handle the complexities of modern multi-tenant organizations. It provides a robust suite of tools for managing people, organizational structures, and workforce operations with high auditability and flexibility.

## Core Pillars

HAER is built around several key pillars that ensure it can scale from small businesses to large global enterprises:

- **Organizational Flexibility**: Support for multi-level hierarchical structures (L1-L5) and matrix organizations.
- **Data Integrity & History**: Full support for **Slowly Changing Dimensions (SCD2)**, allowing you to track the history of any record over time.
- **Extensibility**: Built-in support for custom fields and entities without code changes.
- **Workflow & Automation**: Streamlined processes for attendance, leave, and performance management.

## Key Modules

### [Organization Structure](/product/organization)

Manage your company's hierarchy across five flexible levels. Define departments, divisions, units, and more, all while maintaining a complete historical record of changes.

### [Employee Management](/product/employees)

A centralized hub for all person-centric data. Track profiles, employment details, and personal information with strict privacy controls.

### [Job & Position Management](/product/jobs)

Define the architecture of your workforce. Manage job roles, positions, and the competencies required for each role.

### [Workforce Operations](/product/operations)

Handle the day-to-day operations:

- **Attendance & Work Schedules**: Flexible shift planning and real-time tracking.
- **Leave Management**: Automated request and approval workflows.
- **Payroll & Salary**: Accurate compensation management.

### [Talent & Performance](/product/performance)

Focus on growth and feedback:

- **Competency Framework**: Define and assess proficiencies.
- **360 Feedback**: Comprehensive performance reviews.
- **Talent Marketplace**: Internal mobility and career development.

## Technical Foundation

- **Multi-Tenant Architecture**: Secure isolation between different organizations and workspaces.
- **GraphQL & REST API**: Flexible integration options for external systems.
- **Audit-Ready**: Every change is tracked, ensuring compliance and transparency.
